About Us

Who We Are

Liberia Safe Environment & Research Services, Inc is a registered multi-sector company based in Monrovia, Liberia. We provide comprehensive environmental services, construction and engineering solutions, research and consultancy, and social development services.

We work closely with government agencies, NGOs, international development partners, private companies, and investors to deliver sustainable solutions that drive Liberia's development agenda forward.

✓ Registered with the Liberia Business Registry

Our Mission

To provide innovative, sustainable, and professional solutions that enhance environmental protection, promote social development, and support infrastructure growth across Liberia.

Our Vision

To be the leading multi-sector service provider in Liberia, recognized for excellence, integrity, and sustainable development impact.
